Facts about Jumia

Jumia is a leading pan-African e-commerce platform founded in 2012, operating a marketplace, logistics network, and digital payment service (JumiaPay) to serve consumers and businesses across numerous African countries, with a mission to leverage technology for everyday convenience and economic growth. The company faced significant operational hurdles but has adapted its model, for instance, by implementing Women and Youth Empowerment Programs to foster trust and build acceptance for online services.
